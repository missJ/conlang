### Resources
---

[The Language Construction Kit](http://www.zompist.com/kit.html)
    basic components of language construction and evolution

[Hergé's Syldavian: A Grammar](http://www.zompist.com/syldavian.html)
    example grammar for comparison purposes

[International Phonetic Alphabet](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Phonetic_Alphabet)
    symbol explanations plus handy chart and further links

[Swadesh Lists](http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Swadesh_lists)
    many language comparisons plus basic 207-word list in English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Dutch, Swedish, & Latin

[Indo-European Lexicon](http://www.utexas.edu/cola/centers/lrc/ielex/PokornyMaster-X.html)
    master PIE etyma list based on book by Julius Pokorny
